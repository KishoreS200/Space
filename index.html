Universe Timeline

A journey through cosmic history

13.8 billion years ago

Big Bang

The universe begins with an incredibly hot, dense singularity that rapidly expands.

13.6 billion years ago

First Stars

The first generation of stars (Population III) form from primordial hydrogen and helium.

4.5 billion years ago

Earth Formation

Our planet forms from the solar nebula, eventually developing a solid crust and oceans.

4.5 billion years ago

Moon Formation

A Mars-sized body collides with Earth, creating debris that forms our Moon.

3.8 billion years ago

Origin of Life

The first life forms appear on Earth, beginning the long journey of evolution.

300,000 years ago

Humans Appear

Homo sapiens evolve in Africa, marking the beginning of modern human history.

70,000 years ago

Modern Humans

Behavioral modernity emerges with advanced tools, art, and complex social structures.
